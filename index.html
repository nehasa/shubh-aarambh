About Me

Compassionate, Evidence-Based Care

I am a Rehabilitation Psychologist specializing in child development, behavioural interventions, neuropsychological assessment, and family-centred rehabilitation.

My work integrates scientific assessment, empathetic counselling, and structured therapeutic planning to help individuals achieve emotional, social, and cognitive well-being.
